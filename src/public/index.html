CONNIE | Film | 3 Minutes

A divorced couple meets in a rented apartment to act out a bizarre sexual ritual.

Connie is a snapshot into one of the unfeeling sexual liaisons between John and Vi, a divorced couple. The cold, business-like nature of their meeting in an abandoned apartment juxtaposed by the intimate yet provocative style of their love making, exemplifies the level depravity and loneliness to which their relationship has sunk.
